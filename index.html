Questions ?

BAM Seminar @ Cannes 2025

How to get to the airport?
We recommend you to carpool to the airport and leave the car at the Interparking. Keep your ticket and submit it afterwards via your expense note.
When and where will we meet?
We will meet at the Servier desk from Brussels Airlines at Zaventem at 9am.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in times are as follows: Monday & Wednesday: 6 PM
Check-out time for all guests is on Friday before 11 AM
For your convenience, a room will be available to store your luggage upon arrival and after check-out on Friday. Even though you won't be leaving immediately, your luggage will be safely stored until your departure.
What are the flight times and flight numbers?
Monday 22 September -> flight Brussels-Nice SN3617 11:00-12:50
Wednesday 24 September -> flight Brussels-Nice SN3617 11:00-12:50
Friday 26 September -> flight Nice-Brussels SN3622 16:30-18:15
What is the baggage allowance?
The drop off baggage allowance is 23kg per person, hand luggage is 8kg.
How will I get to the hotel from the airport?
Upon arrival in Nice, there will be a transfer service by bus to take you to the hotel in Cannes.
What is the dress code for the seminar?
During the day: Casual and comfortable
Gala evening (Wednesday night) : Gala outfit & bow tie.
What is the theme of the BAM?
The theme is BECOMING PREMIUM, the seminar is our chance to shine together and achieve excellence.
Is a lunch provided on Friday?
No, there is a free lunch on Friday. You can claim reimbursement through an expense note for a maximum amount of 50€.
